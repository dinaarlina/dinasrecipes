The ingredients needed to make Easy orange chicken:
  1. Prepare for the sauce:
  2. Prepare orange juice
  3. Take sugar
  4. Get white vinegar
  5. Prepare soy sauce
  6. Make ready garlic powder
  7. Get ground ginger
  8. Prepare corn starch
  9. Get water
  10. Take red chili flakes (optional)
  11. Take Orange zest from one orange
  12. Take Prepare fried boneless wings (see my recipe)
  13. Make ready Garnish: green onions (optional)
Instructions to make Easy orange chicken:
  1. Prepare chicken nuggets/ boneless wings (see my recipe)
  2. In medium sauce pan combine juice, vinegar, soy sauce, ginger garlic and chili flakes and cook on medium for 3 minutes.
  3. In separate bowl mix water and cornstarch. Pour cornstarch and water mixture into the orange sauce in the pan and continue cooking 5 minutes until it thickens. Remove from heat and add in orange zest if you wish.
  4. Toss chicken in sauce and garnish with green onions or zest if you wish and serve! Pairs great with fried or white rice and homemade crab rangoons (see my recipe for rangoons)
