The ingredients needed to make Fresh & Easy Shrimp Salad with Lemon, Red Wine Vinegar, & Dill Dressing:
  1. Get medium precooked shrimp cocktail type shrimp
  2. Take English (Hot House) cucumber that's been seeded and cut into 1/4" cubes
  3. Get sweet bell pepper (red, orange, or yellow are good) cut into 1/4" pieces
  4. Take cherry or grape tomatoes that have been cut in half
  5. Take sweet or red onion cut into 1/4" pieces
  6. Make ready chopped fresh dill or 1 Tablespoon dry dill
  7. Get fresh lemon juice
  8. Get Red Wine Vinegar
  9. Take salt
  10. Get sugar
  11. Take olive oil
Steps to make Fresh & Easy Shrimp Salad with Lemon, Red Wine Vinegar, & Dill Dressing:
  1. Put all ingredients in a mixing bowl except for oil. Toss thoroughly and adjust seasoning if needed, then add oil and toss again. - - That's it! Told you it was easy. :)
  2. Enjoy for brunch or as a light, refreshing snack or meal!
