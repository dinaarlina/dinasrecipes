The ingredients needed to make Santa Fe Stuffed Peppers:
  1. Get 1 lb ground turkey
  2. Prepare 1 medium onion, chopped
  3. Make ready 3 clove garlic, minced
  4. Take 1 can red beans with chili seasoning, drained
  5. Get 1 can salsa-style fire roasted tomatoes
  6. Make ready 1 packages Simple Truth Organic Red quinoa and brown rice (frozen)
  7. Make ready 1 tbsp chili powder
  8. Prepare 2 tsp ground cumin
  9. Prepare 6 tbsp mild taco sauce
  10. Get salt and pepper
  11. Get 4 oz pepperjack cheese, shredded, divided
  12. Get 4 large red, yellow or orange peppers
Instructions to make Santa Fe Stuffed Peppers:
  1. Prep peppers by cutting off top and removing stems, seeds and membranes. Boil water and submerge peppers for 3 minutes. Set aside. Preheat oven to 350.
  2. Brown turkey, onion and garlic. Add beans, tomatoes and rice/quinoa mix, taco sauce and spices and stir together thoroughly. Let simmer 7-10 minutes then stir in half the cheese.
  3. Place peppers in 2 qt baking dish and stuff with meat mixture. Top with remaining cheese and taco sauce. Bake 15 minutes til cheese is browned and bubbly.
